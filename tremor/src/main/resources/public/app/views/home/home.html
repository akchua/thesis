A few words
from us

We are your very first web-based tremor application. We use an advanced technology, which is the Leap Motion Sensor, in order to quantify your hand tremors without contact. Leap Motion Sensor captures every millisecond of movement there is and then it is processed in order to give you the amplitude and frequency of your hand tremor. This data would then be visible to your doctor. Your convenience and satisfaction is what makes us going.

- TremorPro Developers
